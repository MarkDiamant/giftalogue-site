How it works

Giftalogue works with a limited number of brand partners per issue. We do not operate an open‑submission model.

All placements are designed in‑house to ensure a consistent, premium look throughout the catalogue.

Placement formats

Placement formats vary by issue and availability, and include:

  • Single product features
  • Multi‑product pages
  • Editorial / themed spreads
  • Premium positions (covers and key sections)

Availability

Availability is limited per category and per issue. Full details, including pricing and timelines, are provided in the private media pack.
